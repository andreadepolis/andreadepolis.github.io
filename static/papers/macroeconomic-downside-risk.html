Modeling and Forecasting Macroeconomics Downside Risk

with Davide Delle Monache and Ivan Petrella
Journal of Business & Economic Statistics (2023)
We model secular trends and cyclical changes of the predictive density of US GDP growth and document a substantial increase in downside risk over the last 30 years. Conditional skewness moves procyclically, implying negatively skewed densities ahead of recessions and positively skewed distributions in expansions.
