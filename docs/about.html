Welcome to my research website

I am a Research Economist within the Monetary Policy and Euro Area Economy division at the Banco de España. My research interests include monetary economics, macroeconometrics, and Bayesian econometrics. I received a Ph.D. in Finance and Econometrics from Warwick Business School, at the University of Warwick.

Before joining the Banco de España, I worked in the Quantitative Investment Strategies team at Fulcrum Asset Management and I was a Senior Economist at NowCasting Economics Ltd.
