The Taming of the Skew: Asymmetric Inflation Risk and Monetary Policy

with Leonardo Melosi and Ivan Petrella
Working Paper (2024)
Inflation risk in U.S. data varies significantly over time and often exhibits asymmetry. We introduce an econometric model with time-varying asymmetric risk that improves forecasting accuracy and formalize the resulting policy wedge within a DSGE framework, deriving implications for optimal monetary policy.
