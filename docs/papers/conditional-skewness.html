Testing for Conditional Skewness with Epsilon-Skew-t Distributions

Working Paper
Abstract: A parametric LM test detects instability in the third moment of time series using the flexible Epsilon-Skew-t distribution. Monte Carlo evidence documents appropriate asymptotic properties and applications highlight non-Gaussianity in asset pricing anomalies. Our approach provides a powerful test for detecting changes in conditional skewness that is robust to various forms of misspecification. The test is based on the score function of the Epsilon-Skew-t distribution and can detect both gradual and abrupt changes in skewness. Applications to financial time series reveal significant evidence of time-varying skewness in asset returns, particularly during periods of market stress.
